Level 1:

In level 1 you will find yourself in the forest. In this forest there will be 3 riddles you have to solve to get to the other end. If you answer the riddles correctly then you will be able to go onto the next level, if you get one riddle wrong then it will regenerate and give you another riddle. Have fun!

Level 2:

In level 2 you will find yourself in a part of the Arctic. You will be given tasks and a riddle to complete throughout the way and once completed you will be allowed to go to the next level. When you complete the task there will be a riddle that follows, if you get the riddle correct then you will be allowed to go to the next task. If you get riddle wrong, then it will regenerate a new one for you to answer.

Level 3:

In level 3 you will find yourself in space. You will have to complete 5 tasks foloowed by a riddle. When you complete the task there will be a riddle that follows, if you get the riddle correct then you will be allowed to go to the next task. If you get riddle wrong, then it will regenerate a new one for you to answer.
